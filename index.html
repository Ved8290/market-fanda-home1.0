Our Services

Welcome to Marketfanda, the ultimate solution for businesses seeking comprehensive sales solutions. At Marketfanda, we specialize in providing our clients with end-to-end sales services .

Lead Generation

Lead generation is the process of identifying and cultivating potential customers for your business. This includes techniques such as email marketing, social media outreach, and targeted advertising to attract new prospects to your company.

Learn More

Direct Sales

Direct sales involves selling products or services directly to customers without intermediaries, such as retail stores or distributors. This can include strategies such as door-to-door sales, telemarketing, or online sales.

Learn More

Salesforce Development

Salesforce development involves customizing and optimizing the Salesforce CRM platform to meet the specific needs of your business. This includes developing custom workflows, creating custom fields, and integrating third-party applications to improve the functionality of the platform

Learn More

Business Development

Business development involves identifying new opportunities for growth and expansion in your business. This can include researching new markets, developing new products or services, or building strategic partnerships to drive growth.

Learn More

Advertising

Advertising involves promoting your products or services to potential customers through various channels such as print, radio, television, or online media. This can include creating targeted ad campaigns to reach specific demographics or using retargeting strategies to reach customers who have previously engaged with your brand.

Learn More

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing involves promoting your products or services through digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and other online platforms. This can include techniques such as search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, content marketing, social media marketing, and email marketing.

Learn More

Public Relations

Public relations (PR) involves managing the relationship between your business and the public, including customers, investors, and the media. This can include strategies such as media relations, crisis management, reputation management, and event planning to build and maintain a positive image of your brand in the eyes of the public. PR can also involve creating and distributing press releases, organizing press conferences, and building relationships with journalists and influencers.
